About Us

We are a group of passionate and dedicated Soldiers and Airmen within the Missouri National Guard at Jefferson Barracks.

Our members travel from multiple states to architect, develop, and train with true cyber professionals that believe that people are more important than hardware, quality is better than quantity, operators cannot be mass produced, operators cannot be created after an emergency, and that operators must operate as a team.

The Missouri National Guard Cyber Team architected, engineered, developed, and operationalized the Response Operation Collection Kit (ROCK) as well as the Cyber Analytics Platform and Extraction Sensor (CAPES).

If you are ready to join the team, in or out of uniform, please contact us - we're always ready to learn and collaborate.
